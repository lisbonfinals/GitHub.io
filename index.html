The system

Automated from
detection to post

Six autonomous layers — from raw radio signals to 4K Instagram posts. No human in the loop. Real-time ADS-B decoding, AI vision scoring, neural upscaling and intelligent scheduling — all running 24/7 on a Raspberry Pi 4 under the Lisbon sky.

01 📡
Detect & Measure

A RTL-SDR dongle decodes ADS-B transponder signals at 1090 MHz — capturing callsign, altitude, speed, heading and ICAO code for every aircraft within 5 km and below 5,000 ft. Simultaneously, a USB condenser microphone monitors ambient noise in dB SPL. Only when both conditions are met — aircraft in zone and noise above threshold — does the system trigger.

RTL-SDR ADS-B 1090 MHz readsb dB SPL 5 km · 5,000 ft
02 👁
Capture & Score

A 4K dual-lens camera streams via RTSP. Every captured frame is scored by a YOLO v8 neural network — only frames exceeding 0.30 confidence are selected. The best frame then goes to Claude Haiku, which analyzes livery, aircraft type, registration, route context and atmospheric conditions to generate a natural-language caption.

Reolink 4K RTSP YOLO v8 Claude Haiku 0.30 threshold
03 ✦
Enhance & Publish

The selected photo is upscaled 4× to near-4K resolution using Real-ESRGAN ONNX (SRVGGNetCompact), running on the Pi CPU — approximately 15 minutes per photo. It is then queued with a calculated publication slot, respecting night silence windows (23h–7h) and daily post quotas. Published autonomously to @lisbon.finals via the Instagram Graph API with the full Haiku-generated caption and ADS-B data.

What's next

Upgrades in progress

The station is continuously evolving. New hardware and sensors are being integrated to capture even more — from vibration analysis and industrial-grade acoustics to live ATC communications and air quality monitoring.

🔊
130 dB Sound Sensor

A calibrated GAIN EXPRESS industrial sound meter connected via USB serial, replacing the current condenser microphone. Accurate measurements up to 130 dB SPL — no more sensor saturation during the loudest events. Real data, real numbers.

Hardware · Ordered
🌀
Dual IMU Vibration

Two MPU-6050 accelerometers on a GTIWUNG breadboard (I²C addresses 0x68 + 0x69) will measure structural vibration and infrasound from passing aircraft — a data layer entirely invisible to acoustic sensors. Useful for low-frequency impact analysis.

Hardware · I²C
🌬
Air Quality Station

PMS5003 (PM2.5 / PM10 fine particles) and SGP30 (VOC / CO₂ equivalent) sensors will correlate aviation kerosene exhaust with particle counts over the terrace — a first for residential aviation monitoring in Lisbon.

Environmental · SGP30 · PMS5003
📡
Live ATC Listening

A second RTL-SDR dongle tuned to 118.1 MHz (LPPT tower frequency) will capture live air traffic control communications — matching callsigns in real time with clearances, landing instructions, and go-around orders.

RTL-SDR · 118.1 MHz
