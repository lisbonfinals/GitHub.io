The system

Automated from
detection to post

Three layers of technology working in real time, 24 hours a day, from a residential terrace in Lisbon directly under the final approach path.

01 📡
Detect

ADS-B transponder data is received in real time — altitude, speed, heading, callsign, airline. Cross-referenced with acoustic sensors measuring noise levels in dB SPL.

RTL-SDR ADS-B dump1090 dB meter
02 👁
Capture

A 4K dual-lens camera locks onto the aircraft. YOLO neural network validates the frame quality. Claude AI analyzes the image and generates the caption — aircraft type, livery, context.

Reolink 4K YOLO v8 RTSP Claude AI
03 ✦
Publish

The best frame is automatically published to Instagram with a complete caption — callsign, airline, altitude, speed, noise level. Zero human intervention required.

Raspberry Pi 4 Cloudinary Meta Graph API
